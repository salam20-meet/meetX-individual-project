About Our Studio

Rabea Morkus dance studio located in Kafer Yassef, a village in the north of Israel. It is a unique initiative in the Arab sector, which fosters young generation of dancers from several villages in the north of the country. Beside the regular educational activity, the studio is the home of Rimaz Dance Group, a Palestinian dance group founded by Rabeah Morkus in 2007. The group combines traditional Arabic dance rituals with contemporary dance, thus creating a new and unique dance language based on the roots of Palestinian people and everything drives our thoughts and feelings.
